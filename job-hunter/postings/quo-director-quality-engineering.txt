Role Description

We're looking for an Engineering Director who will be the ultimate owner of product quality across all of Quo's product lines — with the authority and organizational backing to enforce standards, block releases, and drive the fixes needed to meet them. The ideal candidate has a strong background in software engineering and test strategy, and a proven track record leading quality programs in fast-moving SaaS environments. They should thrive where initiative, ownership, and high standards aren't just encouraged — they're expected. This is not a purely supervisory role. You will be expected to actively contribute to the activities of the Quality team in all stages of the SDLC. You embrace the Quality Engineering team’s role not only as a check on their peer’s work but also as educators and enablers on testing best practices and methodologies. You actively leverage emerging AI automation technologies to extend the reach of your team.

•
Own quality end-to-end.

•
Define and execute the cross-product test strategy — manual and automated — with daily and weekly validation cycles.

•
Serve as the final release gatekeeper.

•
Build and lead the QA team.

•
Drive cross-functional alignment.

•
Build the test infrastructure.

•
Champion AI-enabled testing.

•
Educate and advocate.

•
Measure and report.

•
Close the customer feedback loop.

•
Explore the edges.

Qualifications

•
You take full ownership of product quality and are comfortable being the person who says "we're not shipping this" — backed by data, clear criteria, and senior leadership's trust.

•
You have a track record of exercising release authority, including blocking or delaying releases, and communicating those decisions effectively to engineering and executive stakeholders.

•
You like balancing hands-on technical oversight with strategic leadership — building both the QA frameworks and the governance structures that give quality decisions real organizational weight.

•
You're a strong communicator who can align quality priorities across engineering, product, and operations without friction.

•
You're data-driven: you define the metrics, track them rigorously, and use them to make decisions and hold teams accountable.

•
You have 7+ years in QA or Software Engineering, with 5+ years in a leadership or managerial role, and proven experience in B2C, SaaS, APIs, or AI/ML platforms.
