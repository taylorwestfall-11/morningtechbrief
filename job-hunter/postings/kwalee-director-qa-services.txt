Join the team!
As we continue to grow, we are now looking for a Director of QA Services oversee and manage the wider QA Services for both internal & external stakeholders across our teams in the UK & India. A big focus for this role will be to create, develop, & establish the QA services as an MSA for external clients converting QA Services from a Cost Centre into a Profit Centre. You will manage the P&L for the department, and establish a long term plan for ambitious growth and profitability whilst ensuring the operational success of the QA function for Kwalee’s internal projects.
Responsibilities

• Strategic & Commercial Vision: Establish and execute the commercial strategy to convert the internal QA function into a profitable, external Managed Service Agreement (MSA).

• P&L Accountability: Manage the departmental Profit & Loss (P&L), ensuring ambitious growth, financial performance, and overall profitability.

• Business Development: Drive and be solely accountable for the business development efforts for QA Services, securing new external client contracts.

• Commercial Negotiation: Develop, agree, and manage all commercial agreements, SLAs, and pricing models with external clients.

• Global Leadership: Provide direct line management to the Head of QA and oversee the success of multi-site QA teams (UK & India).

• Operational Excellence: Maintain high-quality QA standards and strict SLAs for both internal and external projects, ensuring no drop in required quality.

• AI/Automation Roadmap: Build and implement the strategic plan for transforming QA through the increased use of AI and test automation.

• Process Improvement: Upgrade and maintain robust QA processes, SLAs, reporting, and performance dashboards.

• Resource Management: Ensure optimal resource allocation to effectively balance internal game testing needs against external client commitments.

• Executive Reporting: Work closely with the Executive Team to provide strategic updates on commercial performance and growth pipeline.

• Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner with Production, Development, Publishing, and Finance teams to maximise commercial success across the company.

• Final Escalation Point: Serve as the ultimate authority for critical client issues or internal resource conflicts related to QA.
Requirements - Commercial & Strategic Leadership

• Proven track record in business development and commercial management within a service-based business (ideally QA, Technology, or Gaming).

• Demonstrable experience establishing, growing, and managing a profit & loss (P&L) for a business unit or department.

• Expertise in converting an internal cost centre into a profitable Managed Service Agreement (MSA) for external clients.

• Extensive experience in negotiating, finalising, and managing complex commercial agreements, contracts, and Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with external partners.

• Ability to develop and execute a comprehensive commercial strategy for a technical service offering.
QA & Operational Expertise

• Deep understanding of QA best practices, methodologies, and processes in the gaming or software industry.

• Demonstrated experience in upgrading and maintaining global QA processes, reporting, and quality standards across multiple locations (e.g., UK and India).

• Experience in driving the transformation of QA through the use of AI and test automation strategies in collaboration with Engineering teams.

• Strong ability to balance and manage resources effectively between internal project needs and external client commitments.
Management & Collaboration

• Significant experience in senior leadership and line management, including direct management of senior leads (e.g., Head of QA) and indirect management of multi-site, global teams.

• Excellent stakeholder management skills, with the ability to report and present to the Executive Team (COO/CEO) on financial performance and strategic direction.

• A decisive leader capable of serving as the final escalation point for critical client issues and resource conflicts.

• Exceptional collaboration skills, working effectively across Production, Development, Finance, Legal, and Business Development teams.
